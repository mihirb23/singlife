# CareShield Product Reference

## Purpose
Product details for Singlife CareShield Standard (EYA) and Singlife CareShield Plus (EYB). The AI uses this to validate application data and answer product-related questions.

## Plan Codes
- EYA: Singlife CareShield Standard
- EYB: Singlife CareShield Plus

## Eligibility Requirements
1. Life Assured must be covered under basic ElderShield or CareShield Life policy
2. Applicant must be below 62 years old
3. Must be Singapore Resident (resided in Singapore for more than 183 days in the last 12 months)
4. Must be proficient in both spoken and written English
5. Must have attained at least GCE 'O' level or 'N' level certification (or equivalent)
6. This Supplement can also be purchased by ElderShield (ESH) policyholders
7. All Supplements are regulated under the CareShield Life and Long-Term Care Act

## Premium Structure
- Annual premium of up to S$600 is payable by MediSave
- Maximum MediSave deduction: S$600 per life assured per calendar year
- Premium in excess of S$600 must be paid by credit card or cash
- Premium is based on selected plan and entry age
- Benefit Payout Structures: Level or Escalating (fixed escalation rate, cannot change after inception)

## Payment Methods (Section C of Application Form)
- CPF MediSave Account: Up to S$600 per calendar year
- Credit Card (Visa/Mastercard): For premium in excess of S$600
- If policy is backdated, may need 2 premium deductions in same calendar year (may exceed S$600 MediSave limit)

## Application Form Sections
- Section A: Particulars of Life Assured (Name, NRIC, DOB, Address, Nationality, Contact, Income)
- Section B: Health Questions (2 main questions about pre-existing conditions and daily living activities)
- Section C: Premium Payment Method (MediSave + Credit Card split)
- Section D: Declaration (eligibility, switching warnings, data consent)
- Section E: Personal Data Consent (PDPA)
- Section F: E-Documents preference

## Health Questions (Section B) — Determines STP Eligibility
Question 1 - Have you ever had:
a) cancer, diabetes, stroke, AIDS or HIV infection
b) dementia, Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, multiple sclerosis, motor neurone disease
c) heart disease (heart attack, narrowing of heart artery, disease of heart muscle)
d) liver disease (cirrhosis, hepatitis C, liver failure, alcoholic liver disease)
e) kidney disease (chronic kidney disease, kidney failure)
f) lung disease (COPD, sarcoidosis, bronchiectasis)
g) high blood pressure

Question 2 - Have you ever:
a) had conditions requiring medication for >14 days or regular medical treatment
b) had recurring symptoms yet to seek medical advice
c) needed assistance for daily activities (washing, dressing, feeding, toileting, walking, transferring)
d) experienced drop in domestic/social abilities in last 12 months

If ALL answers are No: eligible for STP (subject to Jet UW checks)
If ANY answer is Yes: requires further underwriting review

## 6 Activities of Daily Living (ADL) Definition
1. Washing: Ability to wash in bath/shower
2. Dressing: Ability to put on/take off garments
3. Feeding: Ability to feed oneself prepared food
4. Toileting: Ability to use lavatory or manage bowel/bladder
5. Walking or Moving Around: Ability to move indoors room to room on level surfaces
6. Transferring: Ability to move from bed to chair and vice versa

## Free-Look Period
- 60 days after receiving the policy to terminate if not suitable
- Singlife may recover underwriting expenses

## Important Warnings
- Anyone insured under CareShield Standard/Plus is NOT eligible for Additional Premium Support (APS) from Government
- If currently receiving APS for MediShield Life/CareShield Life, choosing this plan stops APS
- This applies even if someone else is paying for the policy

## Channel: QnB (Direct Purchase)
- FAR Name: DIRECT MARKETING (Source Code: 60003819)
- No financial adviser representative involved
- Customer self-selects plan and premium on website
- Customer declares they bought without advice from FAR
