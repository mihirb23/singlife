## Premium Factors for CareShield Plans

### Factors That Affect Premium
1. **Plan Type**: EYA (CareShield Standard) vs EYB (CareShield Plus)
2. **Entry Age (ANB)**: Age Next Birthday at policy inception — primary pricing factor
3. **Gender**: Male vs Female — different premium tables
4. **Monthly Benefit Amount**: Higher benefit = higher premium
5. **Benefit Structure**: Level vs Escalating (escalating premiums increase annually)
6. **Premium Term**: Number of years premiums are payable (until ANB 69)

### Factors That Do NOT Affect Premium
- Race, nationality, occupation (for CareShield supplements)
- Address, email, phone number
- Income level (though collected on form)

## Premium Payment Split
Total Annual Premium = MediSave Portion + Out-of-Pocket Portion

- MediSave maximum: S$600 per life assured per calendar year
- Out-of-pocket: Paid via credit card (Visa/Mastercard) or cash

## When Premium Mismatch Occurs (Step 6A)
Possible reasons for premium amount not matching between L400 and Application Form:
1. **Birthday crossover** (Step 6E): If application is processed after client's birthday,
   ANB changes and premium changes
2. **Backdating** (Step 6F/6G): If policy is backdated to before birthday, premium should
   use pre-birthday ANB
3. **Gender/DOB error** (Step 6H): If Gender or DOB was entered incorrectly in Lives
   Assured page, premium will be wrong
4. **Campaign code** (Step 6H): Promotional discounts may affect premium
5. **GST changes**: Premium includes GST — rate changes affect total

## Birthday Crossover Logic (Step 6E)
- ANB = Age Next Birthday = current age + 1 (on or after birthday in the year)
- If application submitted before birthday but processed after birthday:
  - ANB increases by 1
  - Premium increases to next age band
  - Backdating may be needed to use original lower premium

## ERI Check (Step 6F)
- ERI = Earliest Risk Inception
- Rule: Basic plan start date must be BEFORE the backdated/inception date
- If invalid: Block Decision — cannot proceed until dates are corrected

## Payment Mode Validation (Step 6I)
Check that the payment mode on L400 matches Section C of the PROPF (Application Form):
- Billing Frequency: Annual (01), Semi-Annual (02), Quarterly (04), Monthly (12)
- Billing Channel: B = Bank (GIRO), D = Direct (Credit Card/Cash)
- MediSave + Credit Card split must match form declaration
