====================================================================
RCS CHECK EVALUATION GUIDELINES
====================================================================

When Claim Indicator = Y in a case, the RCS (Risk Claims System) check
evaluates the ICD codes against the policy terms:

HIGH RISK (likely refer to UW):
  - C80 (malignant neoplasm)
  - F32/F09 (mental health conditions)
  - I51 (heart disease complications)
  - E14 (diabetes)

MODERATE RISK (may refer to UW depending on severity/context):
  - E78 (cholesterol — most common, often leads to exclusion)
  - I10 (hypertension)
  - K75 (liver disease)
  - J45 (asthma)
  - I77 (arterial disorders)

LOWER RISK (often standard with possible exclusion):
  - M54 (back pain — most common overall)
  - D36/D24 (benign neoplasms)
  - H58/H93 (ear/eye)
  - L30 (dermatitis)
  - N73 (pelvic inflammatory)
  - Z83 (family history only)
  - R07 (chest pain — symptom only)

NOTE: This classification is derived from observed data patterns in the
NB.MASTER file. The actual pass/fail determination should be confirmed
with the UW team (Sharon/Terence) for production use.
