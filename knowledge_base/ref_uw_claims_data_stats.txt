## Key Data Columns Relevant to AI System
### For QA Review (Use Case 3):
- ICD_CNT, ICDS, EXC_CNT, EXCS, LOADING1_MAX, Reopen Cases Flag
- DAYSTOISSUE, UWID, UW_OUTCOME1, UW_OUTCOME2

### For Email Drafting (Use Case 2):
- Claim Decision, Type of Claim, UW_OUTCOME1
- Condition Grouping (NOT to be disclosed to customer)

### For Analytics (DSA Prompters):
- All claim fields, BMI data, Disclosure data, Channel, F2F indicator
- Early Claim flag, AGE_NEXTBDAY, Product Group
